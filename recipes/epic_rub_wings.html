Epic Dry Rub Air Fryer Chicken Wings!

Gwen's twist on these epic wings!

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 20 mins

Total: 30 mins

Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings (as many as you want!)
  • Epic Dry Rub (recipe below)
  • Olive oil

First, we need to make our Epic Dry Rub!

Dry Rub Ingredients

This amount will coat about 10 full size wings (20 half wings)

  • 1/2 Tbsp ancho chile pepper
  • 1/2 Tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 Tbsp onion powder
  • 1/2 Tbsp kosher salt
  • 3/4 tsp chili powder
  • 3/4 tsp paprika
  • 3/4 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp dried ground thyme

Process

  1. Pat your wings dry with a paper towel
  2. Lather lightly with olive oil before sprinkling the epic rub over the wings. Feel free to use your hands to evenly coat the wings with spice.
  3. Flip the wings and spice the other sides before placing them in your air fryer.
  4. Set your air fryer to 400 degrees Fahrenheit and let the wings cook for 11 minutes
  5. Flip after 11 minutes and let them fry for 8 more minutes.
  6. Let cool and enjoy!
