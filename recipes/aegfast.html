Gwen's Everyday Ægfast!

Prep Time: 5 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Total: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 Everything Bagel
  • Cream Cheese
  • Wife Spice

Process

  1. Set a medium sized greased pan to heat on medium heat (4/10)
  2. When the pan is heated, crack and begin cooking both eggs, season with Wife Spice. Do not break yolks.
  3. Once the yolks are only slightly jiggly, flip the eggs and season the other side with wife spice. Put the bagels to toast.
  4. After 3-4 minutes, flip the eggs again and turn off the heat. Leave in the pan as you add cream cheese to the bagels.
  5. Lightly sprinkle some Wife Spice onto the cream cheesed bagels, then serve the eggs
  6. Enjoy!

Gwen eats this meal Neurospicy Style:
  1. Choose the worse looking bagel and eat it first
  2. Like a gremlin, seperate the first egg yolk from the white (yes, with your hands). Eat the white and sop up the extra Wife Spice with the yolk before you eat it
  3. Repeat with the second egg, and revel in perfection as you finish by eating the second half of the bagel.
(Deviating from this may not give maximum food enjoyment)
