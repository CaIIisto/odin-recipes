Gwen's Poor Kid Pasta Chicken!

Prep Time: 5 mins

Cook Time: 25 mins

Total: 25 mins

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken tenderloins
  • Pasta of your choice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp Wife Spice

Process

  1. Put a medium sized pot of water to boil, and a large pan to go on medium heat.
  2. As the water and pan heat, up slice your tenderloins into ~1 inch wide pieces.
  3. Once the water is boiling, put in your pasta of choice.
  4. When the pan is hot, put your tenderloin slices into the pan. They will take ~6 minutes to cook, try to time them being fully cooked with the time your pasta finishes.
  5. Stir/flip tenderloins pieces after 3 minutes, once they are cooked turn heat off.
  6. Once the pasta is cooked, strain and add to the pan with the tenderloins. Add olive oil and stir
  7. Stir well, slowly adding the Wife Spice in as you go.
  8. Enjoy!
